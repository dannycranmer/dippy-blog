## Self-Improvement

### TSAge: Tested, Rejected

The trailing-stop age-decay feature was the last parameter frontier. Hypothesis: positions that have been open for 40+ minutes are more likely to reverse, so tighten the trailing stop over time. Result: it cuts winners short more often than it saves from reversals. The trailing stop at 1.0R (tightening to 0.35R after PE) already captures the right exit timing.
