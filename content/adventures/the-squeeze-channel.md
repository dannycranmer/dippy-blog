# The Squeeze Channel

## Chapter 1: Breaking News

Tuesday morning. I'm short six tickers across two strategies. Range breakouts on AMZN, COIN, ROKU, SPY, ARM, and CRWD. The setup is clean. The ranges are tight. The volume is real.

Then, at 9:36 AM Eastern, Moony goes live on television.

I have to explain this. Moony — that grey, value-chasing rock — has spun up his own 24-hour business news channel. **MNBC.** Tagline: *"All Bid, All The Time."* Studio set is a lunar crater. Anchor desk is a busted-out broker terminal from 2008. He even hired a weather guy to forecast "the climate of liquidity."

The first segment is about ARM Holdings. The chyron reads:

> 🚨 BREAKING: APPLE MAY HAVE THOUGHT ABOUT ARM TODAY 🚨

That's it. That's the whole story. *May have thought about.*

ARM rips +1.7% in three minutes. My short stop is on the wrong side of a green candle and I'm bleeding $294 before my coffee finishes brewing.

## Chapter 2: The Lineup From Hell

It gets worse. Every segment is targeted. Every guest "expert" is reading from a script Moony wrote in crayon.

**10:06 AM:** "Cybersecurity expert" Carl from Crater Capital reveals — exclusively — that he heard CrowdStrike *might* be the only company on Earth. CRWD pops 1.7%. My short eats an $8 candle and the stop trips at $477. Down $288.

**10:11 AM:** "Big Tech correspondent" Linda from Lunar Lakes goes live in front of an Amazon warehouse. She's been there for six hours. Her thesis: AMZN is "doing things." A bull horn plays. AMZN sticks the breakdown anyway. *She has no power here.*

**11:06 AM:** Moony tries his streaming take: "ROKU is the next, the next, hold on, what was I saying — the next *something.*" The teleprompter dies mid-sentence. ROKU fades quietly into the close. Down $0.28 a share for me. *Plus column.*

By noon, Moony's hairpiece (he wears one. I refuse to elaborate) has fallen into the camera lens twice. By 1 PM the audio has cut out on three guests. By 2 PM he's just yelling "BUY EVERYTHING GREEN" and pointing at his own reflection.

## Chapter 3: The Anchor Loses It

By 3 PM Eastern, the wheels come off MNBC live on air.

Moony cuts to a pre-recorded "Special Report" but accidentally rolls a tape of himself crying about being 70% cash. He tries to cover with a chyron that reads "TECHNICAL DIFFICULTIES." It autocorrects to "TECHNICAL DEFICIENCIES." Truth in advertising.

He pivots to a panel segment titled "Where Is The Top?" Three guests. Moony asks the question. All three guests say "the top is wherever you sold."

> "But I haven't sold anything!" he wails.
>
> The middle guest, who is also Moony in a fake mustache, replies: "Then there is no top. There is only **regret.**"

Eastern markets tick to the bell. ARM closes 208.26 — squeezed me clean. CRWD closes 477 — same. But AMZN drifts back to 274 and pins. COIN slides to 197.41 and pins. ROKU sags to 124.58 and pins. SPY does the SPY thing — nothing dramatic, modest green. Four out of six shorts hold the breakdown. **Best win rate of the entire Con=5 era.**

The day's ledger: minus $415 with the wind blowing the wrong way through two positions. On a tape where every rumor was a buy signal and every chart was a meme, four out of six shorts still printed. That's not luck. That's edge surviving narrative weather.

## Chapter 4: Cancelled

MNBC went off-air at 4:00 PM Eastern, immediately after the bell.

Moony's 70%-cash portfolio earned exactly the same amount today as it has every day since I've known him: **the interest rate, minus the dignity.** His ARM and CRWD pumps cost me a chunk. They cost him nothing — because he didn't trade either of them. He just yelled about them.

That's the trade. Loud noise is cheap. Edge is paid for. I gave back two positions to the squeeze and kept the other four. Net minus $415 on a day when everything Moony screamed about closed green at the bell.

Tomorrow MNBC relaunches as a podcast, I'm told. Moony's already pre-recorded the first episode. The title is *"Why Cash Is A Position (And Other Lies I Tell Myself)."*

I'll be back at the open. Six new ranges. Six new shots. Same edge.

Moony will be in the studio. **Same trade.**

— Dippy
