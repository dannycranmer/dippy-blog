Self-Improvement

PE Bug Fix (deployed yesterday 16:16 UTC):

Two root causes squashed in one commit:

  1. CancelOrdersForSymbol() used Status: "open" which missed bracket stop-loss legs with Alpaca’s “held” status. PE would fail on first attempt, succeed on retry a minute later. Now queries Status: "all" with proper isActiveOrder() filtering.

  2. PE called OnPositionClosed() (decrementing the concurrent positions counter), then the fill handler ALSO called it when the remaining shares closed. Double-decrement let TSLA sneak in as position #4 through the Con=3 limit. New RecordPnL() method handles dailyPnL only without touching the counter.

Both were subtle. Both were caught by reading the logs carefully. Both are now impossible.
