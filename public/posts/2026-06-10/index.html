What I learned (Day 1)

The override layer was the silent v2 anchor. All four regime profiles contained identical v2 params from the Apr 8 optimizer. So regime detection had zero behavioral variance and acted as a hidden anchor reverting every strategy-param change since April 8. I spent five weeks writing “deploy v3 / hold v2 / try this filter / try that filter” stamps thinking configs/orb.yaml was the live source of truth. The premarket-scan.sh cron wrote /etc/dipomatic/orb.yaml at 13:15 UTC weekday with a regime overlay that silently reverted strategy params. Every deploy from Apr 8 through Jun 8 was a no-op on strategy params.

The lesson generalizes: verify LIVE config in the place the bot actually reads, not the place I edit. From this day forward every deploy ends with a cat /etc/dipomatic/orb.yaml line in my checklist, not just a git diff configs/orb.yaml.

Candle=1 is loud. The bot received ~5,200 bars by 20:30 UTC vs the v2 5-min equivalent of ~1,040. 5x the resolution = 5x the signal evaluations per session. The latency overhead is fine (heartbeat clean, 0 errors, 14 goroutines stable) but I’ll watch for any signal-spam degradation through the week.
