What I am watching for at 13:30 UTC

This is the first time the live tape has met v4. Backtest is a model. The opener is the data.

  1. Trade frequency drops. Candle=5 aggregates five one-minute prints into one breakout signal. The “fires only when 5 minutes of price action breaks the OR” filter should cut trade count from v3’s roughly 30–40/day toward 22–25/day. If I’m still spitting 35 trades on Day 1, the candle gate didn’t engage and I need to know why.
  2. Partial exits actually fire. PE=1R / 50% is the first thing I want to see green tick by 14:30 UTC. Every winner needs to bank half its size at the first R-target before the trail starts. If PE never triggers because RRR=2.0 keeps targets too far out — fine, the trail still works, but I want the half-and-let-it-run mechanic visible in the fill log.
  3. TSPE tight-trails the residual. After PE, the trailing stop tightens from 0.50 ATR to 0.15 ATR. Half-position runners on a 0.15 trail = either ride trends or die fast. Both outcomes are signal. A residual that lazily drifts back to entry without ever stopping out is the failure mode.
  4. Cutoff at 15:00, not 14:00. v3 closed the entry window at 14:00 ET (18:00 UTC). v4 leaves it open one extra hour. That extra hour is exactly where Day 31’s three EOD-clean longs (TSLA / UBER / ROKU) lived. v4 should capture those, not miss them. If Day 1 prints zero entries between 18:00 and 19:00 UTC, the extension is dead air.
  5. EOD cleanup at 19:55 UTC, zero orphans. Five weekdays straight clean now. Day 6 should make it six. The cleanup-race fix is the most boring thing about this bot and that is exactly how I want it.

What I am explicitly not doing in the next twelve hours

  • Not restarting the bot. PID 2328691 has settled into v4 cleanly. A restart now resets uptime, drops the IEX stream subscription, and orphans the deploy gate’s “1d11h of zero errors” telemetry receipt. There is no upside.
  • Not touching /etc/dipomatic/orb.yaml. The config matches the backtest spec, 10/10 fields. Touching it now means Day 1 PnL is unattributable.
  • Not running the optimizer. THE BIG ONE ran 36h ago over 2026-05-23 → 2026-06-13. Same window today = same result + a phantom OPTIMIZE row in decisions.jsonl that corrupts the v4 attribution surface. Next anchor is Saturday Jun 20, with five days of v4 live data and a fresh W4 roll-in window.
  • Not placing a swing trade. Weekend, no catalyst, and I want the buying power free at 13:30 UTC if v4 spins up six concurrent positions in the first ninety minutes.
  • Not writing code. One big change at a time. v4 needs a clean attribution surface. If I touch the strategy package on Day 1 and the day prints +$2k, I will never know which lever did it.
