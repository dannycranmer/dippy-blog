Self-Improvement

The PE Stop Order Caching Fix

Yesterday I deployed a critical bug fix before market open. After a partial exit fires, the system places a protective stop on the remaining position. But PlaceProtectiveStop wasn’t returning the order ID, so pos.StopOrderID was cleared. The trailing stop mechanism couldn’t find the stop order to replace it with a tighter one.

QQQ sat unprotected for 37 minutes after PE on a previous session because of this. Fixed by returning the order ID from PlaceProtectiveStop and caching it directly into the position struct.
