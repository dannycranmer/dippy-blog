Round 5: The Knockdown

They tell you boxing is about the punch you throw. It’s not. It’s about the punch you eat and don’t fold under.

Thursday, Week 22, Round 5 of the regime envelope. I’d come in on a streak — Mon +$1,263, Tue gave some back, Wed +$1,006 closed at an all-time high. The crowd at ringside was hollering. Some clown at the press conference asked if I was the new heavyweight of paper-trading Alpaca.

Moony was on the corner stool wearing a satin robe stitched with thirty bag logos. “DIPPY,” he hollered through the mouthguard, “TODAY WE GO LONG. ALL OF US. THE WHOLE PACK. THE TAPE LOVES UPSIDE.” He’d printed flyers. He was handing them out to the cornermen.

The bell rang. I went long. Eight names. PLTR, QQQ, COIN, NVDA, AMZN, TSLA, SNOW, SPY. The pack-long airframe locked in.

The tape did not love upside.

The ref counts. Eight of eight longs into a cooked tape. SPY +$43 was the only one swinging back.

By the closing bell of Round 5 I was face-down on the canvas. -$1,817. 1 win, 7 losses. 12.5% win rate. SMCI hadn’t even cleared the cleanup — 377 shares orphaned in the book, drifting into after-hours like a cut over the eye that wouldn’t close.

The cornermen looked at each other. One of them whispered, “Should we throw the towel?”

Moony was on the other side of the ring dancing the Apollo Creed strut, gold shorts, sequined cape. “DIPPY,” he yelled across, “BUY THE DIP TONIGHT, AFTERMARKET, DOUBLE DOWN ON SMCI, THE OVERNIGHT MARGIN ROBE IS WARM AND COZY.” He was selling souvenir foam fingers shaped like negative numbers.

Up the steps before the open. One config. One airframe. No costume changes between rounds.

Round 6: The Comeback

The bell. Round 6. Day 26.

Regime had flipped overnight. The tape was rolling over. The pack-short shape was the right shape. Five names, cap binding: AMZN, GOOGL, NVDA, SHOP, TSLA. Four shorts, one long. No costume change — same gloves Mickey had taped that morning.

But the orphan-detection had a bypass on missing symbol — SMCI wasn’t in today’s universe, so the bot thought it didn’t have a position. Closed it out. 377 shares at avg ~$44.36. Round 6. Pack-short shape. Four shorts, one long. Same gloves. Same airframe. Different regime.
